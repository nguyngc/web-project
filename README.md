# iVision
## Description
iVision is a modern web platform designed to simplify eye care services. It allows users to easily book appointments with doctors for vision checkups, while doctors can access patient information, manage schedules, and provide digital prescriptions after examinations. Administrators can manage user accounts, oversee website content, and ensure smooth operation of the platform.

## Target Users & Stakeholders:
iVision is designed to serve three main groups of users:

- Customers / Patients:
Individuals who want to book appointments for vision checkups, consult with eye specialists, and receive digital prescriptions. They can also browse clinic information, access updates, and manage their personal profiles online.

- Doctors / Eye Specialists:
Healthcare professionals who manage their schedules, view upcoming patient appointments, access patient details, and provide prescriptions after consultations. The platform helps doctors streamline their daily workflow and improve communication with patients.

- Administrators:
Admin users oversee the overall system, including managing user accounts, doctor profiles, and website content. They ensure that all data, appointments, and information remain up-to-date and consistent across the platform.

## Main Functionality:
- For Customers:
  - View available time slots
  - Book and manage eye checkup appointments online
  - View appointment / prescriptions history
  - Manage personal profiles online

- For Doctors:
  - View patient appointments and profiles
  - Record diagnoses and issue prescriptions digitally
  - Manage availability and schedule

- For Administrators:
  - Manage user and doctor accounts
  - Update clinic information, news, and announcements
  - Oversee appointment data and maintain website content
